Incident checklist

  1. Confirm impact scope (services, regions, % errors).
  2. Establish timeline and last known good deploy.
  3. Identify top error signatures + blast radius.
  4. Mitigate (rollback, feature flag, traffic shift) with confirmation.
  5. Notify stakeholders and update status page.
